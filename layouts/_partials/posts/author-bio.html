About the Author

AJ Djalali is Research Faculty and Senior Research Engineer at the Georgia Institute of Technology. He graduated Cum Laude from Northwestern University and was a Fulbright Fellow at the University of Amsterdam before earning his PhD in Linguistics from Stanford, where he worked on formal semantics and mathematical linguistics.

In industry, he built the natural language technology behind Tableau's Ask Data, holds 9 patents in natural language interfaces for data visualization, and led cross-cloud AI strategy at Salesforce. In 2023 he co-founded PerceptivePanda, an AI startup acquired by Zapier in 2025.

Outside of work, he rides a 2015 Indian Scout, collects guitars, and hunts for rare vinyl pressings.
